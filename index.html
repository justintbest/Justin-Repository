About

Crafting form
from nothing

Justin Best is a digital sculptor working at the intersection of mathematics and visual art. Each piece begins as an equation and ends as something you can hold with your eyes.

Working primarily in 3D space, the focus is on how light interacts with geometry — the way a surface catches and releases illumination, and how that creates an emotional response.
